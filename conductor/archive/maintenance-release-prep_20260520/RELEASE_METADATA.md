## Subtitle
Offline-first clipboard manager with encryption

## Description
ClipVault is a native macOS clipboard manager designed from the ground up for privacy, security, and speed. It lives quietly in your menu bar, automatically saving everything you copy—text, images, files, and links—so you never lose a snippet again.

Unlike other clipboard managers, ClipVault is built with a zero-trust architecture. It operates entirely offline, using the strict macOS App Sandbox to ensure it cannot transmit your data anywhere. Every item in your history is encrypted on your hard drive using AES-256-GCM, and is only decrypted in memory the moment you view it.

**Key Features:**
- **Encrypted History**: Your data is locked down with AES-256 encryption.
- **Privacy First**: Fully sandboxed, zero network access, and completely offline.
- **Rich Previews**: View beautiful inline previews for Markdown, code syntax, images, and files.
- **Smart Search**: Find exactly what you need instantly with optimized, as-you-type search.
- **Sensitive Auto-Purge**: Automatically detects and deletes sensitive strings (like credit cards) after a customizable timeout.
- **Keyboard Driven**: Navigate, search, pin, and paste entirely from your keyboard.
- **Native Polish**: Designed specifically for macOS with fluid animations and a beautiful frosted glass aesthetic.

Take control of your clipboard history without compromising your privacy.
